Whether you are from a developed country or underdeveloped country, Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. In present days we can observe the state of the education domain and drastic changes that have been taking place in approach of teaching. The Present e-learning frameworks are changing rapidly with the change in technology and need in education. In the present education process, there are many problems that Instructors and students face but one of the main problems is unavailability of effective learning models and collaborative tools for complex topics , especially in science courses that are either not practical or not technically feasible in the real world or simply beyond the limitations of the real-world. This is due to lack of visualization in learning platforms which is one of the major problems that needs focus now, otherwise we might face many problems related to falling short of potential in giving effective teaching in near future. This paper is a research which is made on Lack of visualization methods issue and gives an idea on how to solve this problem by testing it through various phases and forms of evaluation. The survey details and referred research paper details are included in here. We surveyed the use of visualization methods in practice and recorded the results and will carry our research based on the results obtained..
